About Teacher Resource Platform

Teacher Information System shall facilitate an end-to-end management of teacher lifecycle. For the entire lifecycle, a teacher has multiple entry, exit points and instances from the education ecosystem. A unique number, associated with a teacher, will be used to map the entire journey of a teacher spanning across different stages of teacher life. This will ensure teacher records created can be issued to the correct individual with consented access. Effective implementation of ICT in various processes such as Teacher Administration, Transfer , Vacancy Management, Linkage of PMIS etc. with Teacher can not only bring in efficiency in the overall service delivery of the school education department but also the much-required transparency in the processes.
